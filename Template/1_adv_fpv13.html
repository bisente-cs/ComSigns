How to say:

I need to charge my phone

Point your index finger at your chest. Use your dominant hand: Form your dominant hand into a "B" handshape (with fingers extended and together). Place your dominant hand in front of your chest, palm facing down. Bend your elbows and bring your hands down slightly while keeping your palms facing down. This motion represents the concept of needing or wanting something. Extend your index finger with your dominant hand to form a "V" shape, then move it towards your index finger. Next, place your flat hand on your chest. After that, using your dominant hand, extend your little finger and thumb to make a phone sign, and then place it to your ear.
