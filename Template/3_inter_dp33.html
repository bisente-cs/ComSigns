How to say:

Month

Begin by forming both hands into the American Sign Language (ASL) number 1 shape. With your non-dominant hand, have your index finger pointing upward and the palm facing outward. At the same time, position your dominant hand in the same shape, but with the index finger pointing toward you. Once your non-dominant hand is upright, use your dominant hand to make a horizontal movement.
