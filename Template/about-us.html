COM SIGNS

Welcome to COM SIGNS, a pioneering force in the realm of communication and learning technology. At the heart of our mission is a commitment to addressing the unique challenges faced by the Deaf-Mute Community. In a world driven by technological advancements, we recognize the transformative potential of our Interactive American Sign Language Translator and Instructional Device

OUR MISSION

To bridge the communication gap and enhance learning opportunities for the Deaf-Mute Community by developing cutting-edge technology integrated seamlessly into a specialized website platform.

OUR VISION

To empower Deaf-Mute Individuals by revolutionizing communication and learning experiences through innovative technology, fostering inclusivity and breaking down barriers

INTRO DUCTION

In an era marked by rapid technological progress, our journey began with the realization that Interactive Hand Gesture Translators and Instructional Devices hold the key to transforming communication for individuals who are deaf-mute.

Despite their profound impact, these tools often go unnoticed, leading to a lack of recognition within certain communities.

Understanding the persistent challenges faced by Deaf-Mute Individuals in utilizing technology for communication, we set out to create a system that addresses issues related to understanding spoken language, limited awareness of Sign Language, and the absence of real-time translation solutions.

Driven by a creative vision, we embarked on a mission to integrate interactive hand gesture translation and educational features tailored to the specific learning needs of the deaf-mute community. Our focus is not just on modules and tutorials but on creating a blended learning experience that converts detected hand gestures into text or sentences.
